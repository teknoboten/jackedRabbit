Intro to Dungeons & Dragons

A welcoming space for young adventurers to enter the world of Dungeons and Dragons (D&D)!

This series will teach newcomers how to play by guiding them along their first quest. Players will learn the rules, solve problems & collaborate with their party members to defeat villains and monsters. Everything they need to get started will be provided!
