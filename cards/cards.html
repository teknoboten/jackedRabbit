Learn D&D for Teens

Date: March 17

Time: 1:00pm - 4:00pm

Ages: 13 - 18

Instructor: Dan

Price: $50

Are you ready to try your first game of Dungeons & Dragons? Join us on this adventure and we'll show you the ropes! Learn to Play D&D: Introductory Adventure is meant to be a one-time primer to get some experience before joining our upcoming D&D campaigns. One of our Game Masters will guide you through the rules in various scenes that highlight the three pillars of Dungeons & Dragons – Social Encounters, Exploration and Combat. This 3-hour session is for teens ages 13 to 18, and is the perfect opportunity to gain the experience needed to join other D&D programs we offer both during spring break and after school! Note: you do not need to come to this adventure with a character - we will have one ready for you.

Magical Kitties Save the Day

Date: March 19

Time: 1:00pm - 4:00pm

Ages: 8-12

Instructor: Sean, Fiona

Price: $50

Step into the enchanting world of Magical Kitties Save the Day, a role-playing game where adventure, mystery, and fun await! Fantasy Craft: Enchanted Fairy House

Date: March 21

Time: 10:00am - 12:00pm

Ages: 5-7

Instructor: Fiona, Jen

Price: $35

Let your child’s imagination soar in our Fairy House Workshop! In this 2-hour crafting event, young creators will design and build their very own magical fairy houses using a variety of colorful materials, natural elements, and plenty of creativity. This hands-on, fun-filled activity allows children to create a whimsical world of their own, complete with tiny details that bring their fairy houses to life.
