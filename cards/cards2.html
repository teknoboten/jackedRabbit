Dungeon Master Academy

Date: March 25 - 27

Time: 9:00am - 12:00pm

Ages: 10 - 14

Price: $150

Step behind the Dungeon Master screen and discover the secrets of crafting exciting campaigns, running quests, and guiding your friends through unforgettable stories. Join our experienced Dungeon Masters for a three-day workshop where D&D players develop their skills as a Dungeon Master! Participants will:

  • Learn DM skills like world-building, encounter design, combat strategy, and storytelling
  • Create adventures, go in depth on gane mechanics and practice running game sessions
  • Develop leadership, communication, decision making and problem-solving skills in a fun, interactive settin

No experience as a Dungeon Master is necessary, but participants must be quite experienced playing D&D. Content will be suitable for both new and experienced DMs. If you have questions, please email info@arrowwoodgames.com.

Fantasy Craft: Magical Pony Makeover

Date: March 27

Time: 2:00pm - 4:00pm

Ages: 7 - 12

Price: $35

Step into the world of medieval horsemanship (without an actual horse) in this hands-on, imagination-filled, equestrian demo! For a few hours, kids will become squires-in-training as they prepare our horse model for a grand adventure. Children will have the chance to get creative as they decorate the dummy, while also learning authentic techniques and using real equestrian gear. Fantasy Craft: D&D Miniature Painting

Date: March 28

Time: 9:00am - 12:00pm

Ages: 8 - 14

Price: $50

Unleash your child’s inner artist in our painting workshop! In this 3-hour session, young artists will dive into the world of painting the miniature figurines used in Dungeons & Dragons, learning techniques to bring small-scale figures to life with color, detail, and creativity. Whether they’re new to the craft or have painted miniatures before, this event will guide them through the basics of painting, from base coating to adding fine details, all while allowing them to personalize their own miniatures. Perfect for young creators interested in art, fantasy, and tabletop gaming.
