D&D One-Shot Adventure (Group 1)

~ For experienced players, playing level 2-4 characters

Date: March 24

Time: 1:00pm - 4:00pm

Ages: 10 - 14 years

Instructor: Mitch

Price: $50

Let your child dive into an exciting one-shot Dungeons & Dragons adventure designed for experienced players that are comfortable playing a level 2 to 4 character.

A few notes for this class:

  • Some prior D&D experience is required. Your child must be comfortable playing a level 2, 3 or 4 character.
  • If your child has come to one of our Learn to Play programs, they will be fine in this adventure.

During this three-hour adventure, children will team up, solve puzzles, battle monsters, and use their creativity to shape an epic story.

Players should bring their own dice, and should come with a level 2 to 4 character created following our guidelines. We will have characters available for kids who need them.

Dungeon Master Academy (Group 1)

~ For experienced players. No experience as a Dungeon Master required.

Date: March 25 - 27

Time: 9:00am - 12:00pm

Ages: 10 - 14 years

Instructor: Dan, Jen

Price: $150

Step behind the Dungeon Master screen and discover the secrets of crafting exciting campaigns, running quests, and guiding your friends through unforgettable stories. Join our experienced Dungeon Masters for a three-day workshop where D&D players develop their skills as a Dungeon Master!

Dungeon Masters don’t just manage the mechanics of the game - they lead a group of players through a variety of situations. Participants will practice decision-making, communication, and teamwork skills that they can use in and out of the game.

  • Learn DM skills like world-building, encounter design, combat strategy, and storytelling
  • Create adventures and practice running game sessions
  • Develop leadership, communication, decision making, and problem-solving skills in a fun, interactive setting

We recommend that your child has participated in at least a few after school campaigns or equivalent. No experience as a Dungeon Master is necessary, and content will be suitable for both new and experienced DMs. If you have questions, please reach out to us.

D&D Epic Battles (Group 1)

~ For experienced players, playing level 4-5 characters

Date: March 26

Time: 1:00pm - 4:00pm

Ages: 8 - 11 years

Instructor: Sean

Price: $50

Get ready for an action-packed time of Epic Battles in the world of Dungeons & Dragons. In this 3-hour event, adventurers will team up to face legendary monsters in a thrilling stand-alone boss fight. If your child enjoys in-game combat, this would be a great fit.

A few notes for this class:

  • Prior D&D experience is required. Your child must be comfortable playing a level 4 or 5 character. Most kids who have come to two or more of our after school programs will be fine in this epic battle!
  • Because of the fast pace of this program, we suggest that kids play a species and class combination that they are already comfortable with.
  • This is an event where characters are likely to die, so we encourage children to create “disposable” characters for this event! Please prepare your child in advance for this possibility.

Players should bring their own dice, and a level 4 or 5 character created following our guidelines.

Fantasy Craft: Magical Pony Makeover

Date: March 27

Time: 2:00pm - 4:00pm

Ages: 7 - 11 years

Instructor: Gabrielle

Price: $35

Step into the world of medieval horsemanship (without an actual horse) in this hands-on, imagination-filled, equestrian demo! For a few hours, kids will become squires-in-training as they prepare our horse model for a grand adventure.

Children will have the chance to get creative as they decorate the dummy, while also learning authentic techniques and using real equestrian gear. Led by a professional equestrian coach and member of the Arrow Wood team, this adventure is perfect for fantasy and horse lovers alike!

Important Note: The horse model was created on a farm with animals (including horses!) If your child has allergies, this may not be a suitable activity for them.

Fantasy Craft: D&D Miniature Painting

Date: March 28

Time: 9:00am - 12:00pm

Ages: 8 - 14 years

Instructor: Mitch

Price: $50

Unleash your child’s inner artist in our painting workshop! In this 3-hour session, young artists will dive into the world of painting the miniature figurines used in Dungeons & Dragons, learning techniques to bring small-scale figures to life with color, detail, and creativity.

Whether they’re new to the craft or have painted miniatures before, this event will guide them through the basics of painting, from base coating to adding fine details, all while allowing them to personalize their own miniatures.

Perfect for young creators interested in art, fantasy, and tabletop gaming.

Open Play in the Games Room

Date: March 28

Time: Beginning at 12:30pm

Ages: All ages (kids under 14 must be accompanied by an adult)

Price: $15 per table per hour (for up to 4 players or a single family)

Our cozy Games Room is open for play!

Did you know that you can drop into our Games Room (when other events are not scheduled) and play any of the games in our library? We’ve got over 200 games, ranging from kid and family games, to party games, to complex strategy games. We’ve got something for everyone.

At $15 per table per hour, this is affordable fun for families looking for an indoor activity.

Our Games Room is always available during regular store hours when other events aren't happening, even when it isn’t Spring Break. And we’re open until 10pm on Fridays.

No need to purchase tickets in advance. Just drop into the store!
